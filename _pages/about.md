<b style="font-size:30px ;">Hello,</b> 
I am <strong style="font-size:20px;">Dipen Shome (Shourav)</strong>, from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I graduated from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology, country's top and highly competitive Engineering university, with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering.
<p style="text-align:justify; text-indent:20px; font-size:16px;">Professionally, I bring over eight years of engineering experience. I started my career as a <strong style="color: #191970;">Design Engineer </strong>in the Research & Development (R&D) Team of a renowned Power System Equipment Manufacturing Company in my country. In this role, I developed my skills in design of Mechanical Components, specializing in the utilization of cutting-edge 3D Mechanical Design and Engineering Simulation Software such as <strong>SolidWorks, Ansys Workbench and PTC Creo.</strong>
</p>Currently, I am working as <strong style="color:#191970;">Lead Operations Engineer (Turbine)</strong> (more than 6 years)  in my country's first <strong style="color:#BB2649;">Nuclear Power Station</strong>. In this role, I have worked extensively in Operation and Maintenance of large-scale rotating machinery (i.e. Steam Turbine, Generator, Pumps) as well as thermal equipment (i.e. Condenser, Heat Exchanger) and plant reliability operations, gaining substantial hands-on experience into equipment behavior, failure mechanisms, and operational diagnostics. Working in a Nuclear Power Station is a challenging but incredibly rewarding role as well. Every day, I am involved in ensuring safe and effiecient operaion of plant's process system equipment crucial for power generation. It requires a blend of technical knowledge, analytical thinking and attention to detail - something that I found both stimulating and deeply satisfying.</p>
<p style="text-align:justify; text-indent:20px; font-size:16px;">Over the past several years, I have developed a growing interest in application of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technique to solve Mechanical Engineering challenges from the perspective of <strong>Industry 4.0</strong>. My current research focus include application of AI for <em> Predictive Maintenance </em> of rotating equipment, <em>Industrial Inspection </em>, <em>Additive Manufacturing</em>.
<p style="text-align:justify; text-indent:20px; font-size:16px;"> As I continue to navigate the challeges of ever-evolving engineering landscape, my commitment remains unwavering - <strong style="color: #0072B5;">to drive progress, inspire innovation, and contribute meaningfully to the intersection of scrience and creativity.</strong>
